About Us

Hey! We are Matthew, Eyosyas, Ali & Shadab and we are first year computer science students. Our app is called Flow. We built the ultimate student go-to app.
Flow helps students keep track of assignment and homework deadlines and makes students aware of up and coming quizzes and tests. Flow also lets students calculate how much they'd need in the next assessments to reach their target goal. It uses real-time data from course websites and updates automatically and organizes all the necessary information about the courses they are taking. Flow helps create a sense of urgency for the student by showing them when they next due date is and inspires them to organize their valuable time more effectively.
